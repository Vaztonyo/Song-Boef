West Coast rapper known as G Perico for his gangsta rap music. He gained recognition for his hit mixtape, Shit Don't Stop. He was born Jeremy Nash in South central Los Angeles, California. He was an only child. His uncle ran an independent record label.

Vince Staples

Vincent Jamal Staples is an American rapper from North Long Beach, California. He is a member of the hip hop trio Cutthroat Boyz. Staples was once a close associate of Odd Future, in particular Mike G and Earl Sweatshirt. He is currently signed to Blacksmith Records, ARTium Recordings and Def Jam Recordings.
